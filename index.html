About Me

Learning new things is one of the best ways to spend your time. This is my latest endeavor. I have been intrigued by computers my whole life. I've been learning to code for the web through a program called Codergirl since January. This was initially a way to get on a new career path while recovering from an illness. Along the way I have met, worked with, and enjoyed the company of an amazing, diverse group of incredible women. I am so glad the stars aligned for me to have this experience. My path has been forever altered by Codergirl. I look forward to getting some more practice working on this site, building one for my husband, and eventually my father's business. The goal right now is to get a few more tools under my belt in order to become a full stack developer and somewhere along the way I'd also like to learn iOS and Android app development.

I also love to work with my hands and create things. I currently work in furniture engineering. I work with designers to make sure their designs will function properly and uphold our standards. I've designed furniture to custom build for my own home and refinished old pieces too. I enjoy art and architecture, and anything beautiful.
